Engineering

This is our design to change the city block given to us. Our goal was to make the most enviornmentally friendly option possible, while creating a safe and welcoming community.

  • Problem Statement

Problem Statement

Improve ammenities and experience of shopping while reducing impervious surfaces and accounting for autonomous vehicles.

Who Wants What?

City Officials

The City Officials knew they needed more housing, and they also provided the main Design Requirements.

Current Residents

The current residents cared about having a more pleasant shopping experience.

New Residents

The new residents needed houses to move into.

Workers

They were interested in a carless community and also a generally more pleasent experience.

Solution Effectivness

Impervious surfaces

We had an 81% reduction of impervious surfaces from 451 units2 to 190 units2 We achieved this with a combination of using porus concrete, and replacing much of the pavement with grass. The only impervious surfaces were the roofs of the buildings.

Happiness of the stakeholders

We achieved the goals of every stakeholder along with other benifits like a ferris wheel.
